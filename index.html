ABOUT US



'Inovus Labs'​ is a Student Community at Kristu Jyoti College of Management and Technology. It was later recognized as an Innovation and Entrepreneur Development Centre (IEDC) by Kerala Start-up Mission in the year 2015. Inovus Labs is a Start-up incubation Centre for nurturing the spirit of Entrepreneurship and Innovative thinking of its fellows. We are the first of its kind at an Arts & Science College. We are funded for our innovative start-ups by Kerala Start-up Mission. Amazon Web Services, KASPEROB, Mozilla, Github, and Internshala collaborate with us to achieve our goals. Technical Support for all the programs conducted in the college are provided by us. 'Come & Explore your !deas @ Inovus Labs'​
